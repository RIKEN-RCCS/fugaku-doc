CONVERGE - Flow to Start Trial Use :red:`(Discontinuation of provision)`
========================================================================

The step to start a trial use of CONVERGE on Fugaku are as follows.

#. Application for Trial Use
#. Notification of Approval and Usage Period
#. Submit of Agreement
#. Start of trial use
#. End of trial use

.. image:: FIGURES/converge_trial.png
   :width: 5.25138in
   :height: 3.23871in

1. Application for Trial Use
-------------------------------

#. Access the request form
   (https://fugaku.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) of Fugaku
   Support Site.
#. Select the "Request form" pull-down menu.
#. Select "Trial use of commercial application" from the "Inquiry type" menu.
#. Input the “Project ID”, “Subject” (e.g. CONVERGE trial use) and “User name” forms.
#. Select "CONVERGE" from the "Application name" menu.
#. Put the information of the users in the "Supplement" field (see below).
#. Check the “Agreed to the precautions” box in the "Notice" section.
#. Push the "Submit" button.

**[Filling in the "Supplement" field]**

Please put the information in the field as follows.

- **Local accounts of the users**

  The listed accounts will be permitted to access the software
  during the period of the trial use. Please put the local accounts of 
  all the users.

- **Details of your computation**

  Describe as much details of your computation as possible in any format.

- **Preferred start date**

  The period of the trial use is one week. Put the preferred start
  date in yyyy/mm/dd format. Please consider the following points to
  determine the date.

  - It takes \ **at least twelve working days** \ between the submission of
    the application and the start of the trial use.
  - You have to make and sign an agreement document (see below) and
    submit it two working days before the start of the trial use. \
    **Please take into account the days required to prepare the
    agreement.**\
  - Check the schedule of Fugaku operation because periods of
    the maintenance or the large scale job execution are included in
    that of the trial use.
    System schedule: \ https://www.fugaku.r-ccs.riken.jp/en/operation_schedule

  We might not be able to meet your request about the start date for
  some reasons. You can request some additional preferences about the
  period. We will take into account them to determine the period but
  might ask you to give us other possible ones.

  [ Example of requesting the start date ]::

   (Mandatory)
   - First-preferred start date: yyyy/mm/dd
   
   (Optional)
   - second-preferred: yyyy/mm/dd, third-preferred: yyyy/mm/dd
   - excluding the period from yyyy/mm/dd to yyyy/mm/dd
   - no preference

- **End date of your project**

  Put the end date of your project that applies the trial use in yyyy/mm/dd format.

- **Information of a representative**

  Put the following information of a representative user, who may not
  be the applicant. He or she have to submit the agreement (see below).

   User information
    | Affiliation:
    | Department and position:
    | Name:
    | Address of the department:
    | Phone number:
    | Email address:

- **Note**

  The period of the trial use cannot be extended but can be added 
  if you submit an application again after the current period ends
  and the vendor approves it. When you submit an application again,
  provide us how many times do you applied the application, how long
  period do you need, and why do you need the additional period, as
  well as the information you provided at the first application.
  Please describe as much details of these information as possible, 
  because the vendor use them to judge if the application is approved
  or not.

**[Conditions of Use]**

This trial use program is only for evaluating the performance and
usability of CONVERGE to determine if you start the actual use of it
on Fugaku. Before starting the trial use, you have to sign the
agreement document "7-DAY SOFTWARE EVALUATION AGREEMENT" (see below)
and submit it to the vendor after the period of the trial use is fixed.

In addition, your user information will be provided to the vendor in
advance because you should purchase a normal license of CONVERGE for
the actual use of it on Fugaku.

For this purpose, R-CCS will provide the vendor with the following
information including your personal one that is provided at the
application.

For now, only inhabitants of Japan who use the software in Japan can
apply for this trial use program.

Note that the trial use might not be approved by R-CCS or the vendor.

	**[Information to be provided]**

	- Department
	- Position

	- Email address
	- Details of your computation

2. Notification of Approval and Usage Period
----------------------------------------------

R-CCS and the vendor check the application and notify whether the trial use is
approved or not. If approved, the usage period is also notified.

Please note that the usage period of your first choice is not always
approved. If the period is not convenient for you, tell us that with
the procedure on the notification.

After the usage period is notified, proceed to ”3. Submit of
Agreement” as soon as possible.

3. Submit of Agreement
------------------------

Please submit the trial use agreement of the software as follows. The
trial use are not approved until the vendor receives the agreement.

**[How to submit]**

1. Download "7days CVG EVATUATION AGREEMENT for Fugaku via
   Riken.pdf". Please use the latest one because the content might be
   changed without notice.
2. Confirm the content of the agreement and fill in each of the
   fields. Please note the following points.
   
 - Provide the information of the representative registered at the application.
 - Fill in all of the fields in English.
 - Put your signature in the SIGNATURE field.
 - Put the date of the start of the trial use in the DATE field in yyyy/mm/dd format
 - Save it in the PDF format.
   
3. Submit the agreement attached to the reply to the notification.

4. Start of Trial Use
----------------------

There is no notification of the start of the trial use. Note that you
cannot use CONVERGE before the start. See below or the notification of
the approval for details on use.

https://www.fugaku.r-ccs.riken.jp/doc_root/en/user_guides/ConvergeGuide/usage_converge_en.pdf

5. End of Trial Use
--------------------

There is no notification of the end of the trial use. Note that you
cannot use CONVERGE after the end. The result of jobs is not guaranteed
that are running or waiting for run at the end of the trial use.
